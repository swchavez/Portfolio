Stephen William Glenn Chavez

Product Manager

Welcome to my page! My name is Stephen Chavez. I am a young professional living in Oxford, Mississippi. I have a passion for dreaming big, helping others, and meeting everyone where they are with a high quality of geniune friendship. I graduated Cum Laude at the University of Mississippi in 2018 and then graduated with my M.B.A. Summa Cum Laude at the University of Memphis in 2021.

I received my B.A. in Public Policy Leadership and began the life of campaign management. Shortly after serving a national political party in Houston, Texas and in Mississippi, I quickly became intrigued with the technical aspect of campaigns which led me to multiple campaign cycles as Data Director. I served as a Data Director for statewide campaigns in Mississippi and in West Virginia and also did technical campaign work in Baton Rouge, Lousianna. Due to my success as a data director, I then served as campaign manager on multiple campaigns.

I quicly realized how much I enjoyed certain things such as reporting, documenting, and analytics so much so that I went to get my MBA and focused the analytical side of things. Since, I have began a new career path in tech starting as a Systems Analyst at DHL Supply Chain, then as a Professional Business systems analyst at Corelogic. I have since progressed into a role in Product Management serving as the Product Owner for a tier 3 dev ops client experience team for multiple appraisal vendor management softwares and collateral quality control solutions. I am looking at to continue pursuing my goals, growing my knowledge, and dreaming big.

Please feel free to check out my other pages to see my portfolio and how you can reach out to connect! Have a great day!!
